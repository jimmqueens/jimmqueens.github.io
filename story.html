the how...

When creating and designing projects, web design requires familiarity with UX and UI.

A fundamental principle in the design world is: Form follows function. What that means is that an effectively designed thing is:

1. Functional first - it has to be able to perform the said task.
2. Effective in helping users carry out the task - with as least friction as possible.
3. And the form (visual design) of that thing supports the function it is supposed to perform.
In other words - make it usable and then make it pretty. There is another school of thought in design where function follows form but let's keep it simple for now. Let's talk about UX/UI now. UX and UI Design are two of the many sub-divisions under Digital Design.

UI Design: focused on the form - e.g. color, font, arrangement of elements on the screen, etc.

UX Design: focused on the function - e.g. how does a group of screens form a workflow which allows user to do a particular action.1
